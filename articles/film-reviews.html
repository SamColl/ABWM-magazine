Film Reviews

Title: Mary Poppins Returns
Director: Robert Marshall
Starring: Emily Blunt, Lin-Manuel Miranda, Ben Wishaw, Julie Walters, Dick Van dyke



Widowed Michael Banks, is trying hard to keep things together for the sake of his children and his sister. On threat of his house being repossessed, (by the bank he works for) he starts a desperate search for a shares certificate his late father left. Meanwhile, the children are amazed when greeted by a lady, who appears to be flying with an umbrella! Immediately she saves them from imminent danger and tells them she is here to clean them up and look after them, like she did their dad and auntie. Magical trips into days gone by see Mary Poppins and her literal bag of tricks in action! On finding the document and it being 1930 they know that unless they can turn back time they won’t make it! In true Disney style, not only do they stop and rewind time, they save the house and their family. Mary Poppins’ work is now complete! Great film for all ages, catchy songs and laughable antics for all.



Film Fact: Dick Van Dyke appears in the film, only having one role this time, not two like the original!
Length: 130 minutes
Release Date: 23rd December
Rating: PG
