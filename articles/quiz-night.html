What a great night!

The Valentines Quiz on 16 February was a classic example of PPPPPP. Look it up on the Internet if your general knowledge is not up to par. The Quiz Queen was surely Jen Hoskins, ably assisted by her husband Nick “my word is final” Hoskins and Lorraine “is it plugged in?” Anderson.

Answers at bottom of article

Jen planned this event meticulously and as a result, it was a triumph. The questions were varied enough to cater for all ages and persuasions and the delicious food arrived on time in the pouring rain (despite the caterers arriving at St Mary’s Cathedral in Merdeka Square instead of St Mary’s Residences!)

Everyone brought their own drinks and nibbles and we got to know each other intimately in the cosy Function Room at St Mary’s. The questions were loosely Valentine–themed, including a round where we had to guess the identities of Jen and Nick’s allowable celebrity lusts. Björk anyone? Spot prizes were handed out for random acts of cleverness and the answer sheet marking was hotly debated on several occasions.

The final music round ended with a very close finish, with the Dragon Slayers just squeaking in three points ahead of Salty Collins in second place and We’re No Saints in third. The Wooden Spoons went to Anthony and Cleopatra, who took it well! The winning team featured Mel & Ray Bolland, Sally & Bill Addington and Nina & Mark Disney.

I am already looking forward to the Movember Quiz night in November, where Jen, Nick and the KLCC Walkers hope to raise money for Prostate cancer. See you there …
