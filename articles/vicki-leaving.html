Goodbye Vicki

We bid a fond farewell to our patron, British High Commissioner, Vicki Treadell.

Our patron, British High Commissioner Vicki Treadell was welcomed at the ABWM house on Tuesday 5th March for a big thank you and a fond farewell. Vicki is taking up her new post as British High Commissioner to Australia, while she is sad to be leaving Malaysia, is looking forward to all the opportunities that her new role will offer. A small group of us chatted with Vicki and were very amused to hear about some of the packing dilemmas she faces in leaving the embassy to her successor Charles Hay, and his family. It was very kind of Vicki to come to the house and spend time talking with us about some of her experiences as a diplomat in Malaysia.

Cake went down a storm, as it normally does and Vicki left knowing how to cut cake the Facebook way! Thank you Lyn Mansour for educating us all!
